person About My Journey

My journey into data science began during my Master's in Applied Statistics, where I fell in love with the predictive power of mathematics. I realized that equations on a chalkboard could translate into real-world solutions that save money, time, and even lives.

Starting as a Junior Analyst, I quickly learned that clean data is just as important as the model itself. I spent years honing my skills in SQL and Python, ensuring that the foundation of every project was solid. Over time, I transitioned into more complex Machine Learning roles, leading projects that deployed real-time recommendation engines and fraud detection systems.

Today, I mentor junior data scientists and contribute to open-source libraries. I believe in "Explainable AI" — building models that not only perform well but are also transparent and understandable to stakeholders.
